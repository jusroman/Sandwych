We’ve been where you are.

SandwYch was built from the lived experience of patients and families who know how hard it can be to navigate care.

We exist to redesign healthcare so that patients, caregivers, and care teams feel supported, connected, and empowered. Our mission is to create a system where accessing care brings clarity instead of confusion, confidence instead of frustration, and genuine joy in the possibility of living healthier lives.

Our Mission

SandwYch is on a mission to deliver transparent, connected care by uniting secure identity, record matching, navigation, transitions of care, community resources, and cost clarity into one integrated platform that empowers patients, equips providers, and strengthens the systems that care for them.

Our Vision

To redefine healthcare as a transparent, integrated system where patients, families, providers, and health plans are connected through secure identity and unified records, eliminating fragmentation and enabling whole person care for all.
