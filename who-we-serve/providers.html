Providers

How It Works

A Simple, Powerful Extension of Health Care

Consolidate care, eliminate redundancies with data sharing, and task-activate each step of a patient's care plan.

Adherence Support

Your patients’ health outcomes matter now more than ever. SandwYch breaks down your care plans into actionable tasks to ensure continuity of care. Aligned with reimbursement models to meet quality health outcomes.

In-Between Visit Support

Your patients and multi-disciplinary care teams need to stay connected. The SandwYch collaborative platform enhances care planning, patient engagement, and self management to reduce loss of follow-up in between each visit.

Business Support

Your office staff is often consumed by clerical work. By integrating SandwYch into front desk workflows, the check-in process is automated with identification validation and insurance verification to reduce errors and fraud.
