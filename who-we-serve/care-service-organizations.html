Care Service Organizations

Care Service Organizations are the extended network of clinical, behavioral, social, and supportive care providers that surround patients throughout their care journey. These organizations are not just collaborators, they are customers who use the SandwYch platform to manage their own patient panels, coordinate care, and stay connected with enterprise care teams.
