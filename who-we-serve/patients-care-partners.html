Prioritize Health, Together

Managing a chronic condition, disability, or the challenges of aging in place can be overwhelming. No one should have to navigate this journey alone.

The SandwYch app uses a proactive approach with guidance from your primary care provider or care professional to help you gain the tools, clarity, and confidence needed to manage daily health needs.

How It Works

A Layered Care System Built Around You

Support

The SandwYch app allows friends and family to act as an informal Social Circle network and collaborate with patients on their health journey—helping with appointments, communication with providers, and simple tasks.

Assurance

SandwYch is a patient advocate tool for navigating care and managing consent, allowing patients to live as independently as possible while supporting healthcare decision making.

Connection

SandwYch CareCost Map contains a directory of local, ancillary health professionals who are eager to step in alongside your primary health provider. Based on your needs, the app will make suggestions and help you seamlessly connect.
